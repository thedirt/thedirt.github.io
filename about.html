About The Dirt

Between classes, club meetings, on-campus events, and sports games, there's a lot going on around Duke. Venture off campus and into the South's tastiest town and the country's largest research park, and you might be overwhelmed by all there is to do and see.

The Chronicle, Duke's independent daily newspaper, has been serving the Duke community for more than 100 years. Most recently, we've created The Dirt.

The Dirt bridges the gap between your daily newspaper and your favorite blogs. Offering a dynamic take on a traditional newsletter, The Dirt highlights what's trending on and around campus. Whether you're a recent grad hoping to stay in-the-know or a first-year student trying to wrap your head around the Duke craziness, we've got you covered.
